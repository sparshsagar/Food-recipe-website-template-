Pav Bhaji

Mumbai's favorite street food has arrived to tantalize your taste buds! With a spicy vegetable curry that's mashed to perfection and served with soft, buttery buns, pav bhaji is the ultimate comfort food that will transport you straight to the bustling streets of India.

Get ready to indulge in a hearty and satisfying dish that's sure to leave you wanting more!

  • Servings4-6
  • Prep Time20 min
  • Cook Time30 min
  • Total Time50 min

The Ingredients

For Pav Bhaji

Variation: You can add other vegetables like carrots or beans to the pav bhaji mixture.

For a vegan option, substitute the butter with vegetable oil or vegan butter.

The Process

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and butter over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  5. Add chopped bell pepper and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add chopped cauliflower and green peas. Mix well.
  7. Add pav bhaji masala, salt, and mashed potatoes. Mix well and mash the vegetables using a masher.
  8. Add 1/2 cup water and mix well. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the mixture becomes thick and creamy.
  9. Add chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
  10. Heat a griddle or a tawa over medium heat. Slice the pav buns horizontally and place them on the griddle. Toast them until they are golden brown.
  11. Serve the pav bhaji hot with a dollop of butter, chopped onions, and lemon wedges.

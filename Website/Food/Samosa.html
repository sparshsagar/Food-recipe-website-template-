Samosa & Mint Chutney

Get ready to spice up your taste buds with the mouth-watering combination of crispy samosas and tangy mint chutney!

Whether you're in the mood for a quick snack or a full-on feast, this popular Indian street food is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling happy and satisfied.

  • Servings12
  • Prep Time40 min
  • Cook Time20 min
  • Total Time60 min

The Process

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and oil. Mix well.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the dough until it becomes smooth and firm. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. While the dough rests make the mint chutney-
    • Blend together the mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chillies, ginger, lemon juice, cumin seeds, and salt in a blender.
    • Add water as needed to make a smooth paste.
  4. heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan. Add cumin and coriander seeds and let them crackle.
  5. Add green peas, mashed potatoes, turmeric, garam masala, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes until the mixture is dry. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  6. Divide the dough into 6 equal parts and roll each part into a ball.
  7. Roll out each ball into a thin circle, then cut it in half.
  8. Take one half of the circle and form a cone by overlapping the edges. Seal the edges with water.
  9. Fill the cone with the potato and peas mixture and seal the top by applying water to the edges.
  10. Heat oil in a frying pan on medium-high heat. Once hot, add the samosas and fry until golden brown on all sides.
  11. Serve hot with mint chutney.
